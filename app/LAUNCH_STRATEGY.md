# LevelUp - Launch Strategy & Growth Roadmap

**Target Market:** HBO Students (Netherlands)
**Vision:** "Zie je studie-momentum. Bouw discipline. Haal je deadlines."
**Product Stage:** MVP Ready for Pilot
**Business Model:** Freemium SaaS → School Licenses

---

## Executive Summary

LevelUp is a study performance dashboard built specifically for HBO students in the Netherlands. Unlike generic productivity tools, LevelUp combines behavioral psychology (daily objectives, momentum tracking) with gamification (XP, levels) to create sustainable study habits.

**Key Differentiator:** Daily Objectives system that auto-tracks progress and creates clear daily wins, driving retention through habit formation.

## Phase 1: 30-Day Launch Plan (HBO Pilot)

### Week 1: Foundation & Soft Launch
**Days 1-3: Technical Setup**
- Deploy to Vercel/Railway with Supabase EU region
- Set up domain: levelup.study or studielevelup.nl
- Configure production environment variables
- Enable Supabase email confirmations
- Set up error tracking (Sentry)
- Create privacy policy & terms (GDPR-compliant)

**Days 4-7: Closed Beta (10 Students)**

- Onboard with personal walkthroughs

## Freemium Feature Separation

### Free Tier (Retention-Focused)
**Goal:** Get students hooked on core value loop

**Included:**
- Daily Objectives (core habit loop)
- Momentum tracking (this week only)
- Up to 5 active deadlines
- Basic XP and leveling
- 7-day study chart
- Focus timer (manual tracking)

**Limitations:**
- No historical momentum data
- No deadline notes/attachments
- No custom objectives
- Ads in sidebar (subtle)

---

### Pro Tier (€4.99/month or €39/year)
**Goal:** Unlock power user features

**Included:**
- Unlimited deadlines
- Full momentum history (all time)
- Custom daily objectives
- Deadline notes & file attachments
- Advanced analytics dashboard
- Priority support
- No ads
- Export data (CSV)
- Calendar integrations

**Future Premium Features:**
- AI study suggestions
- Pomodoro analytics
- Study group challenges
- Mobile app access

---

### School License (€299/year per 100 students)
**Target:** Student associations, mentorship programs

**Included:**
- All Pro features for students
- Admin dashboard
- Aggregate analytics (privacy-safe)
- Custom branding
- Onboarding support
- Priority feature requests

## Risks & Mitigation

### Risk 1: Low Retention
**Mitigation:**
- Daily Objectives create habit formation
- Push notifications for streaks
- Social proof (leaderboards, challenges)
- Weekly recap emails

### Risk 2: Competitive Threat
**Mitigation:**
- HBO-specific positioning (not generic)
- Speed of iteration (weekly releases)
- Community lock-in (network effects)

### Risk 3: Monetization Resistance
**Mitigation:**
- Start with value (free tier)
- Pro tier is clear upgrade
- School licenses bypass student payments
- Freemium proven in education (Quizlet, Notion)
